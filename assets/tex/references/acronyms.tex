The \ac{iot} connects everyday devices to the internet.
An \ac{led} uses \ac{gpio} pins on an \ac{esp} microcontroller.
Repeated use shows the short form: \ac{iot} devices often communicate via \ac{mqtt}.

\medskip

\begin{description}
    \item[\texttt{\textbackslash ac\{\}}]  \ac{fpga} --- first use expands, then abbreviates
    \item[\texttt{\textbackslash acs\{\}}] \acs{fpga} --- always short
    \item[\texttt{\textbackslash acl\{\}}] \acl{fpga} --- always long
    \item[\texttt{\textbackslash acf\{\}}] \acf{fpga} --- always full
\end{description}
