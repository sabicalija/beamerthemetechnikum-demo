Numeric citation: Sabic introduces the theme in~\cite{demo}.

\medskip

Multiple citations are sorted automatically~\cite{knuth1984,lamport1994,demo}.

\medskip

\begin{description}
    \item[\texttt{\textbackslash cite\{\}}]      \cite{knuth1984}
    \item[\texttt{\textbackslash citet\{\}}]     \citet{lamport1994}
    \item[\texttt{\textbackslash citep\{\}}]     \citep{tantau2023}
    \item[\texttt{\textbackslash citeauthor\{\}}] \citeauthor{knuth1984}
    \item[\texttt{\textbackslash citeyear\{\}}]  \citeyear{lamport1994}
\end{description}
