Ohm's law relates voltage, current, and resistance:
\begin{equation}
    V = I \cdot R
    \label{eq:ohm}
\end{equation}

The transfer function of a first-order low-pass filter:
\begin{equation}
    H(s) = \frac{1}{1 + s \cdot R C}
    \label{eq:lowpass}
\end{equation}

Referencing works as expected: see Eq.~\eqref{eq:ohm} and Eq.~\eqref{eq:lowpass}.
